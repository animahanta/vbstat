GENERAL CRITERIA FOR ADMISSION TO UNDER-GRADUATE [B.Sc. (HONS.)]

1. All candidates seeking admission to under graduate courses should pass the pre-degree or equivalent examination under 10+2 pattern with at least three subjects out of Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Life Science/ Biological Science, Statistics and Computer Science and fulfil the Departmental criteria as shown in Table 1. The admission will strictly be in order of merit of CUET (UG)-2023 score.

2. NTS Scholars: Regular NTS Scholars (Certificate issued by the NCERT, New Delhi) may be admitted directly on supernumerary basis to B.Sc. (Honours), subject to fulfilment of the Departmental criteria as shown in the Table 1.

Admission Criteria

At least 60% marks in Mathematics at 10+2 level or 60% marks in Statistics with at least pass marks in Mathematics at 10+2 level

Category Eligibility Criteria
UR Category

Minimum 60% marks in aggregate and 60% marks in Statistics and pass marks in Mathematics

OR

60% marks in aggregate and 60% marks in Mathematics if Statistics is not offered at +2 level
OBC Category

Minimum 54% marks in aggregate and 54% marks in Statistics and pass marks in Mathematics

OR

54% marks in aggregate and 54% marks in Mathematics if Statistics is not offered at +2 level
SC & ST Categories Passed in Mathematics at 10+2 level

Entrance test

CUET (UG)
